How Do I Create A Reward?

Rewards are a token of appreciation provided by the Creators to thank all those who have contributed to the project. Rewards should be creative, tangible and not overpriced. Rewards should relate back to the project.

For example, if the project is designing and producing a new watch, then one of the rewards could be giving that watch, depending on the Creator's choice of course.

How to create a reward

If your project is a product then your Supporter can receive the product itself. You can even include a special thank you note or a signed letter. Or you can choose to do one or more of the following:

  • Creative Collaboration

    Have your Supporter appear as part of your project.

  • Creative Mementos

    Your Supporter can receive a special thank you gift.

  • Creative Experiences

    Your Supporter could get a chance to participate in an event.

Pricing rewards:

Be sure to offer rewards that will benefit your Supporter as much as you can. Make sure you make it count and keep in mind that Supporters are supporting you because they believe in your project, and not because of your rewards.

Pricing rewards should be done fairly.
